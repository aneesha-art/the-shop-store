Returns & Exchanges

Last updated: April 2025

We offer a 15-day no-questions return policy on all full-price items.

Eligibility

  • Returned within 15 days of delivery
  • Unworn, unwashed, original condition
  • All tags and packaging intact
  • With your order confirmation

How to Return

  • Email returns@theshop.in with order number
  • Team responds within 24 hours with prepaid label
  • Refund processed within 5–7 business days

Refund Timeline

  • Card: 5–7 business days
  • UPI / Net Banking: 2–3 business days
  • Store Credit: Instant, with 5% bonus

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my order? +
Once shipped, you receive a tracking number via email and SMS. Tracking activates within 24 hours of dispatch.
What is your return window? +
15-day return window from delivery date. Items must be unworn with all original tags intact.
Can I change or cancel my order? +
Orders can be cancelled within 2 hours of placement. Email support@theshop.in with your order ID.
What payment methods do you accept? +
All major cards, UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), net banking, and Cash on Delivery under ₹25,000.
Do you offer gift wrapping? +
Yes, complimentary luxury gift wrapping on all orders. Add a personalised message at checkout.
